ABOUT

Welcome to Captchalogue.com! This is a fansite dedicated to the Homestuck franchise and fandom.

Homestuck is a long-running, interactive multimedia webcomp[?] originally published from 2009 to 2017; The fourth in a series of MSPaint Adventures webcomics defined by their user-suggestion driven nature and point and click adventure game satire. Homestuck would use Flash and HTML5 to incorporate games, audio, and animations into its narrative; fully taking advantage of the web as a medium. For more, check out the New Readers Guide.
